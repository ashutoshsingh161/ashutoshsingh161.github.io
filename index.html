About Me

Research & Interests

I am a Ph.D. researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, working on sustainable system development by integration of renewable energy sources into hydropower systems. My research focuses on optimizing hydro-solar-storage systems through multi-objective optimization, exploring the synergies between floating solar PV, hydrokinetic energy, and conventional hydropower. I aim to develop practical, data-driven solutions for enhancing the sustainability and efficiency of existing hydropower infrastructure through intelligent renewable energy integration and AI-assisted modeling.
